Privacy and Safety

LifeRisk360 is designed to support youth safely and responsibly through purpose limitation, role-based access, and safeguarding-aware workflows.

Youth-ready design

Trust and safety by design

Privacy and safety controls help youth participate without turning support into surveillance.

Commitment

Protect youth information, limit data collection, explain purpose, restrict access, and avoid public exposure.

Role-Based Access

Different users have different access levels aligned to responsibility and permission.

Youth Safeguarding

The platform supports awareness, planning, and prevention. It is not a crisis response service.

Data Principles

Collect only necessary information; protect sensitive information; use reports responsibly; apply administrative controls.

Production Controls

Use secure hosting, HTTPS, form protection, access reviews, backups, retention rules, and incident escalation.

Protect youth information and program trust

Apply privacy, safeguarding, access control, and responsible reporting practices from the start.
