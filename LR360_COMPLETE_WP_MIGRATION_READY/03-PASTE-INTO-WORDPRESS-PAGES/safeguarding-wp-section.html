Child and Youth Safeguarding

Safeguarding is central because the platform may involve youth risk awareness, personal goals, stakeholder support, and sensitive information.

Youth-ready design

Safeguarding that protects trust

Safeguarding keeps serious concerns within responsible, controlled support pathways.

Safeguarding Boundary

LifeRisk360 does not replace emergency, medical, legal, school, guardian, or professional support.

Safe Escalation

Concerns should be routed through approved school, parent, guardian, community, or professional channels.

Facilitator Duties

Use respectful language, avoid shame, protect confidentiality, and document concerns according to policy.

Consent and Participation

Use age-appropriate consent/assent and guardian/school approvals where required.

Training

Facilitators should understand privacy, boundaries, escalation, and non-stigmatizing support.

Strengthen youth protection practices

Use safeguarding expectations to guide safe participation, escalation, and responsible adult support.
