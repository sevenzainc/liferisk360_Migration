# Governance

LifeRisk360 governance supports accountability, safeguarding, responsible data use, stakeholder participation, and continuous improvement.

## Strategic oversight
Executive sponsor, program director, advisory stakeholders.

## Youth safeguarding
Named safeguarding lead, escalation procedure, referral boundaries.

## Data protection
Data minimization, purpose limitation, access control, retention, and reporting rules.

## Risk management
Risk review cadence, issue logs, mitigation owners, and improvement actions.

## Program evaluation
Pilot feedback, outcome metrics, sponsor reporting, and decision records.
