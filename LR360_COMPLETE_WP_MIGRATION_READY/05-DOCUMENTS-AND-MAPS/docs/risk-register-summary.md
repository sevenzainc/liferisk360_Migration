# LifeRisk360 Risk Register Summary

Total categories: 15
Total risks: 75

## Personal Safety Risks
Unsafe environments, harmful associations, violence, exploitation, bullying, coercion, or situations where a young person may be unsafe.

## Drug, Vaping and Substance Risks
Exposure to drugs, alcohol misuse, vaping, substance dependency, or pressure to experiment with harmful substances.

## Gang and Criminal Influence Risks
Exposure to gangs, criminal networks, unsafe social groups, or activities that may lead to legal, personal, or physical harm.

## Gambling and Financial Exploitation Risks
Betting, online gambling, gaming-related spending, scams, debt traps, and financial manipulation.

## Sexual Risk and Relationship Safety
Unsafe relationships, exploitation, coercion, pressure, online exploitation, and lack of understanding of boundaries and consent.

## Digital Safety and Cybersecurity Risks
Unsafe online behaviour, weak privacy habits, scams, cyberbullying, identity exposure, account compromise, and digital reputation damage.

## AI Awareness and Technology Risks
Misuse or misunderstanding of artificial intelligence, misinformation, deepfakes, cheating, overdependence, and loss of critical thinking.

## Education and Career Risks
Risks that affect school completion, focus, career exploration, employable skills, and readiness for opportunity.

## Financial and Economic Readiness Risks
Risks that affect money decisions, work readiness, savings habits, scam resistance, and economic independence.

## Emotional and Mental Pressure Risks
Stress, isolation, low confidence, emotional pressure, social comparison, and difficulty coping with life challenges.

## Political and Civic Influence Risks
Political misinformation, polarization pressure, unsafe civic participation, peer pressure over views, and harmful recruitment content.

## Data Privacy Risks
Personal data oversharing, location exposure, app permissions, identity theft, impersonation, and AI/platform data sharing.

## Gender and Identity Risks
Identity pressure, bullying over expression, online identity pressure, respect and boundary gaps, and unsafe disclosure pressure.

## Health and Wellness Risks
Sleep, nutrition, movement, health misinformation, and knowing when to ask for appropriate health or wellness support.

## Social Media Risks
Comparison pressure, viral challenge pressure, oversharing, influencer manipulation, and online conflict escalation.
