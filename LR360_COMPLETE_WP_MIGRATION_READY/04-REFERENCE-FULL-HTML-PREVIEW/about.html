About LifeRisk360

A youth-focused life-risk awareness and economic readiness initiative for practical stakeholder use.

Youth-ready design

Built around real youth contexts

The platform is built for real youth environments: home, school, online spaces, community life, money choices, identity, and future planning.

Mission

To equip young people with risk awareness, life direction, and economic readiness tools that help them make safer decisions and build stronger futures.

Vision

A generation of young people who are aware, prepared, resilient, and equipped to pursue their goals with confidence.

Who LifeRisk360 Serves

Youth

Youth and young adults

Complete assessments, set goals, track actions, and build readiness.

Parents

Parents and guardians

Support safer conversations and approved action planning.

Schools

Educators and facilitators

Run life-skills, digital safety, and readiness activities.

Community

Community groups

Support mentorship, life direction, and readiness workshops.

Sponsors

Funders and partners

Support measurable youth readiness and community impact.

Mitigation

Specialist partners

Provide education, mentoring, digital safety, financial literacy, and referral support.
