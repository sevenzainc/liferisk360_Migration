LifeRisk360

Pilot Program

A practical pilot model to validate usability, impact, safeguarding, and readiness to scale.

Pilot Objectives

Test

Assessment tools

Validate usability, risk language, dashboard workflow, and scoring.

Confirm

Stakeholder reporting

Identify what schools, parents, community organizations, and sponsors need to see.

Improve

Mitigation tracking

Evaluate whether actions, owners, and status tracking are practical.

Measure

Impact readiness

Capture completion rates, awareness improvements, goals, and satisfaction.

Protect

Privacy and safety

Review data minimization, consent, access, and safeguarding controls.

Scale

Funding model

Use pilot evidence to support sponsorship and expansion.

Pilot Phases

PhaseDescription
PreparationDefine scope, participants, safeguarding rules, consent needs, and schedule.
OnboardingRegister users, assign roles, and explain the purpose of the platform.
AssessmentYouth complete guided risk-awareness and readiness assessments.
Dashboard ReviewReview scores, categories, and recommended actions.
Life PlanningCreate goals and connect them to identified risk areas.
Reporting and EvaluationGenerate reports, collect feedback, and identify improvements.

Launch a structured LifeRisk360 pilot

Test the platform with selected youth, schools, parents, community organizations, and partners while building evidence for scale.
