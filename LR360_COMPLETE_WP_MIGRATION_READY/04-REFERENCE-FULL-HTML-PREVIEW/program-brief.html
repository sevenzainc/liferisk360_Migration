Program Brief

Youth-ready design

A practical platform for life-risk awareness

LifeRisk360 connects assessment, dashboards, life planning, stakeholder support, and measurable impact.

Problem Statement

Young people are exposed to increasing levels of risk across social, digital, financial, emotional, educational, and community environments. Many existing systems respond after problems occur. LifeRisk360 focuses on early awareness, prevention, planning, and readiness.

Solution

  • Recognize risks
  • Understand impact
  • Set life goals
  • Create mitigation actions
  • Track readiness
  • Receive structured support

Program Model

  • Website resources
  • Youth dashboard
  • School and community activities
  • Parent engagement
  • Sponsor-supported pilots
  • Partner-led mitigation support

Intended Users

  • Youth and young adults
  • Parents and guardians
  • Schools and educators
  • Community groups
  • Sponsors and partners
  • Mentors and facilitators

Core Outcomes

  • Increased risk awareness
  • Improved decision-making
  • Stronger life planning
  • Economic readiness
  • Safer digital behaviour
  • Better stakeholder support
