Why LifeRisk360

Most youth risks are noticed too late. LifeRisk360 helps make them visible earlier.

Young people are navigating pressure from school, social media, relationships, money, identity, politics, online platforms, privacy exposure, AI, and community influences. LifeRisk360 gives them a simple way to pause, assess, and choose a safer path.

The platform is not designed to shame youth. It helps them understand what is happening around them and build a plan for where they want to go.

How it works

A simple journey youth can understand

LifeRisk360 moves youth from awareness to action in four practical steps.

🧭1. Choose focus areas

Youth select the risk groups that feel relevant to their life and goals.

🎚️2. Rate one risk

Sliders help youth assess likelihood, impact, awareness, support, and readiness.

📊3. Read results

The dashboard shows a risk meter, heat map, priority bars, and risk register.

🛠️4. Build plan

The Life Direction System turns results into goals, actions, and support steps.
