The Life Direction System

The system connects risks, goals, actions, and progress into one personal development pathway. It asks: what future are you building?

  1. Know Yourself
  2. Identify Risks
  3. Set Life Goals
  4. Link Risks to Goals
  5. Create Action Steps
  6. Track Progress
  7. Receive Support
